Vision
Mission statement

Our mission is to bring your ideas alive on the web as quickly as possible in order to maximise the money you spend in regards of what you learn from your customers. Our quality process includes different agile methologies such as XP and Scrum or Kanban. We share our culture, our tools, our knowledge and our passion with our customers.

Transparency

Taking some tools from the Scrum methodology, we daily organize “Standup Meetings” with our customers. Transparency is one of our entrenched values. We share with our customers all our tools and our culture.

Iterative development

Together we define the milestones that the development will go through. We'll setup quickly a first online version, and we'll improve it incrementally with your feedback. With daily or weekly feedback, you will see the advancement of our work. That's the way for your entire satisfaction. It will answer your needs and the needs of your customers.

Sustainable development

This means that our applications and code are sustainable through time, even after their initial development phase. This is our guarantee for cost effective maintenance, now and in the future. That's why we setup a complete production chain : test, staging, production and continuous integration environments.

Development:  environment used by developers, graphic artists etc.
Test:  environment used for unit/functional tests.
Continuous integration:  automatically builds the tests suite.
Staging:  latest code version using production data.
Production:  public facing, live code.

User Interface (UI) / User Experience (UX)

Moreover we now that the Internet is a specific media, that's why Novelys takes care about functional and attractive design.
