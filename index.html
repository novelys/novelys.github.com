We make web & mobile apps.

We are a small team of software engineers crafting innovative web & mobile applications since 2005.

What do we do?

We create websites, web applications, mobile web applications and native mobile applications. We can do it remotely in our office in Strasbourg, France, or we can do it on site, in your office.
Our mission is to bring your ideas alive on the web as quickly as possible in order to maximise the money you spend in regards of what you learn from your customers. Our quality process includes different agile methologies such as XP, Scrum or Kanban.

Why work with us?

We are experts in our domains, and recognized as such. We now have a long track record of successul projects.
The web is our passion, our playing field. We love its plasticity, its constant organic evolution. We are always up-to-date, constantly watching new stuff, experimenting, separating the wheat from the chaff. Therefore, at any moment, we can give you the best answer to your project's needs. We love to say that we prefer at the front of the boat, feeling the fresh wind on our faces.
Transparency is at the core of our company culture: we share our culture, our tools, our knowledge and our passion with our customers.

What are the technologies we use?

We use rapid prototyping technologies to quickly transform your ideas into a live application. Technologies such as Ruby, Rails, Javascript, Amazon Web Services, Heroku.
We then maintain these applications through the time. We work since many years with some customers.
